    Data Warehouse and integrating various sources

    Data Warehouses are centralized super data repositories used to connect many data sources and enable AI and analytics.

    Any AI workflow, from simple automation to RAG, requires a business or organization to have at least a simple data warehouse first.

    Data warehouses don't have to be complicated and large. Not every case requires a Snowflake or BigQuery.

    Individual developers and business can have their own data warehouse out-of-the-box locally!

    In Arkalos, common data sources and connectors like Notion, Airtable, Google Drive and HubSpot are available out of the box.

    config/data_sources.py
        'airtable': {
        'enabled': True,
        'api_key': env('AIRTABLE_API_KEY'),
        'base_id': env('AIRTABLE_BASE_ID'),
        'tables': env('AIRTABLE_TABLES'),
    }

    And let Arkalos

    SQLite, a simple file-based DB, is used as a default data warehouse on your local computer.

    arkalos dwh sync

    And that's it. All data from specific tables will automatically be imported into your own data warehouse.

    And ready for advanced analysis or AI pipelines without the need to download data manually every time.

    Works even offline after data is in your data warehouse!
